Facials & Skin Care

At Salon Hadakari, our facials are designed to make you shine with timeless elegance and modern flair. Each treatment is crafted to complement your skin type and personal needs.

Classic Facial

⭕Purpose: General maintenance, relaxation, and glow

⭕Steps: Cleansing, exfoliation, steam, extraction, massage, mask, moisturizer

⭕Best for: All skin types, especially beginners

⭕Benefits: Improves circulation, removes dead skin, hydrates and refreshes

Oxygen Facial

⭕Purpose: Hydration and rejuvenation

⭕Technique: Infuses pure oxygen and active serums into the skin

⭕Best for: Dull, dry, or aging skin

⭕Benefits: Boosts collagen, plumps skin, gives radiant glow

Chemical Peel

⭕Purpose: Targets dullness and uneven tone

⭕Technique: Uses vitamin C and exfoliants

⭕Best for: All skin types, especially sun-damaged skin

⭕Benefits: Brightens complexion, smoothens texture, reduces fine lines

Hydrafacial

⭕Purpose: Deep cleansing, exfoliation, and hydration

⭕Technique: Uses a patented device to cleanse, extract, and hydrate

⭕Best for: All skin types, especially congested or dehydrated skin

⭕Benefits: Improves skin texture, reduces fine lines, boosts hydration
